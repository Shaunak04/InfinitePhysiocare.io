How to reduce lower back pain safely

A practical physiotherapy article about safer lower back pain relief, movement habits, and rehab basics.

Infinite PhysioCare•6 min read•Updated 2026

Lower back pain is one of the most common reasons people search for physiotherapy. The goal is not to chase pain alone, but to understand what is driving it: sitting time, load tolerance, movement habits, sleep, and weak support patterns.

Start with the basics

Short walking breaks, gentle mobility, and reducing long static postures are usually a better starting point than aggressive stretching. A good assessment should look at hips, spine, and daily activities together.

What a clinic can help with

  • Movement assessment and pain pattern analysis
  • Manual therapy and mobility work
  • Strength progression for the trunk and hips
  • Education for return to work and daily life

When to get checked

If pain keeps coming back, limits sleep, or spreads into the leg, it is worth getting a proper assessment and a clear plan. That is where a structured rehab clinic becomes valuable.
