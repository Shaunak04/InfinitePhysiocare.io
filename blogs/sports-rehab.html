What recovery after sports injury should look like

A physiotherapy blog about recovery phases, strength progression, and returning to sport after injury.

Infinite PhysioCare•6 min read•Updated 2026

Good sports rehabilitation is structured. It should move from pain control to mobility, then to strength, then to sport-specific loading and return to play.

Rehab phases

  • Protect and reduce pain
  • Restore motion and control
  • Rebuild strength and tolerance
  • Progress to sport-specific tasks

Why patients stall

Many athletes skip the middle steps and return too early. A good clinic explains each phase clearly and gives measurable goals so the process feels safe and objective.
